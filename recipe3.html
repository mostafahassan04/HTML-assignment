Caesar Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 large Romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 cup Croutons
  • 1-2 cloves Garlic, minced (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, toss the chopped Romaine lettuce with Caesar dressing.
  2. Add Parmesan cheese and croutons, and toss gently to combine.
  3. If using, add minced garlic and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve immediately to keep the croutons crispy.
